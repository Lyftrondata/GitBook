### Oauth2

This option has been deprecated by Microsoft.

| Key           | Value                       | Field    |
| ------------- | --------------------------- | -------- |

| Client Secret | Your ZohoCRM Client Secret. | Required |
| Redirect Uri  | Your re-directed URL        | Required |
